Our Mission

To build quality accessible homes that improve life for people with disabilities while making profit for our business.

Why Choose Us?

Access Real Estate is Egypt's premier accessible housing developer. We specialize in creating homes with features that exceed standard accessibility requirements by 25%.

Our team includes an in-house Accessibility Consultant and an advisory board of people with disabilities who ensure every design truly serves our community.
